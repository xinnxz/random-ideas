Our Story

A Hidden Gem in the Heart of Bourj Hammoud

Tucked in the vibrant, bustling streets of Bourj Hammoud — Beirut's living, breathing Armenian quarter — Resto Ghazar is more than a restaurant. It's a homecoming.

Every dish is made with pure love, following recipes passed down through generations. The kind of food where you taste the hands that made it — slow-simmered, carefully spiced, and served with the warmth of someone feeding their own family.

Guests don't just visit Ghazar. They return — some every single week when they're in Beirut. Because once you sit at this table, it becomes your table.

— The Ghazar Family
The Menu

Signature Dishes

Every plate tells a story. These are the dishes that keep Beirut coming back.

★ Fan Favorite

Manti

Tiny, handcrafted dumplings bathed in warm yogurt and kissed with sumac. Our all-time bestseller — the dish that built our reputation.

Cabab with Cherry

Succulent lamb kebab braised with tart sour cherries — a sweet-savory masterpiece unique to Armenian cuisine.

Soujouk Cheese Rolls

Flaky pastry rolls filled with spiced soujouk sausage and melted cheese. Golden, crispy, and impossible to eat just one.

Mouhammara

Smoky roasted red pepper and walnut dip, finished with pomegranate molasses and a drizzle of olive oil.

Lahm Bi Ajeen

Paper-thin flatbread topped with spiced minced meat, baked in a traditional stone oven. Available till 4:00 PM — regular, with eggplant, or with pomegranate molasses.

Vospov Keufte

Traditional Armenian red lentil patties, delicately spiced and shaped by hand — a beloved plant-based classic from the old country.

Batata Harra

Spicy potatoes fried to golden perfection, tossed with garlic, coriander, and chili — crispy on the outside, fluffy inside.

Hommos with Meat

Creamy, velvety hommos topped with deeply spiced, pan-seared minced meat and toasted pine nuts.

Kebbeh Sayniyeh

Oven-baked layers of spiced minced meat and fine bulgur — a hearty Armenian-Lebanese classic carved tableside and served warm.

Baba Ghannouj

Silky, smoky roasted eggplant blended with tahini, lemon, and garlic — a timeless, melt-in-your-mouth appetizer.
